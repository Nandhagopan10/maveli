About Me

I am Mahabali, a legendary and benevolent king from ancient times, known for my generosity and devotion in Hindu mythology. My story is closely associated with the festival of Onam in Kerala, where I am celebrated as a symbol of sacrifice and humility. My reign and the tales of my interactions with Lord Vishnu have left a lasting legacy in the hearts and minds of people, reminding them of the values of righteousness and compassion.
